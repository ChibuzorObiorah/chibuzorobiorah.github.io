Copilot on OneNote Canvas

Worked on integrating Copilot, an AI solution, into the OneNote canvas to help users capture their ideas instantly in their context.

OVERVIEW

Microsoft OneNote is evolving to incorporate AI capabilities through Copilot integration. As part of this transformation, we wanted to provide value with the AI capabilities by bringing Copilot to the canvas.

DETAILS

My Role: Led the design efforts
Timeline: 2023-2024
Tools: Figma
Team: Iman Yusuf, Bahvya Kriti, and Jonathan Cheung.

Rewrite

Developed a feature enabling users to utilize Copilot for rewriting their notes in various styles and tones directly on their canvas. This feature leverages AI to provide users with various rewriting options, enhancing their productivity and creativity.

TidyUp

OneNote users frequently struggle with disorganized and poorly formatted notes, which can be tedious to clean up manually. Recognizing this pain point as an opportunity to demonstrate AI's potential in note-taking, I collaborated with an engineer during Microsoft's Hack Week to create a feature that leverages Copilot to automatically organize and format notes, transforming them into clean, readable content.



At the end of the week, we were able to create a working prototype that demonstrated how users could use Copilot to tidy up their notes. It gathered traction from the OneNote team, and it got added into the roadmap for future development.
