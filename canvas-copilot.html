A prototype of the rewrite feature in action, showing how users can select different rewriting options for their notes.

TidyUp

Users in OneNote often have messy and unorganized notes. The work to organize and format these notes can be time-consuming. I saw this as an opportunity to enhance the user experience, and quickly showcase the value of AI in note-taking. So, during Hack week at Microsoft, I teamed up with an engineer to design and develop a feature that would allow users to use Copilot to tidy up their notes, making them more organized and easier to read.

A prototype of the TidyUp feature, demonstrating how users can select and apply different tidying options to their notes.



At the end of the week, we were able to create a working prototype that demonstrated how users could use Copilot to tidy up their notes. It gathered traction from the OneNote team, and it got added into the roadmap for future development.
